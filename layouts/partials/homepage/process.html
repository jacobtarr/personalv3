Process

Like a good home-cooked meal, a good website takes time to make, with proper prep work needed to make the perfect website for your needs. My bread and butter is in building one-page applications and static websites (ones that don't have a backend and are therefore more secure). Below are the stages of what a typical project's life will look like.

My Process

Planning &
Discovery Stage

This is where I sit down with you and understand what your project is about. The target audience, timeline and budget will all be discussed. This is also the phase in which I will brainstorm and research ideas in order to bring your project to life.

{{ partial "svgs/icon-design.html" . }}

Conceptional &
Design Stage

Once I've done the research on the deliverables of your project, it's time to storyboarding your website and laying out visuals. During this time, it's possible revisions can be made before the coding phase based on request.

{{ partial "svgs/icon-development.html" . }}

Development
Stage

Hence the name, this stage is centered around the technical aspects of a website. Markup is created, along with client deliverables. Site functionality is implemented from the site requirements.

Finishing Touches

After testing and ensuring proper support for mobile devices and modern browsers, the site is ready to launch! Things like client training (of the CMS) and any last-minute updates also take place during this phase.
