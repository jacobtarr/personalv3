About

I'm really good at making websites and biking hundreds of miles per week. However, I only went to school for one of those things. I graduated in 2014 with my Masters in Computer Science at Gannon University in Erie, Pa. Fun fact: I earned my bachelor's degree in Journalism before realized I loved designing things for the web. I'm currently a front end web developer in Akron, Ohio for EYEMG Interactive Media Group and moonlight as a freelancer.
